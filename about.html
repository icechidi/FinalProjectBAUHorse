Meet the Team Behind HorseRiders Academy

We are passionate riders and trainers committed to sharing the joy and skills of horseback riding.

Jane Carter – Founder & Head Instructor

Jane has been around horses since childhood. With over 20 years of experience, she specializes in training both riders and horses, emphasizing kindness and communication. She believes everyone, regardless of age, can connect with horses.

“I started this academy to provide a welcoming space where people could learn, heal, and grow through riding.”
